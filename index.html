What is dopplemaker

Doppelmaker is a home replicator you can use to copy yourself. Don’t feel like going to work today? Not enthusiastic about that date tonight? Use Doppelmaker to create an ingenious copy of yourself good for 10 hours (colors and memory fade and your body double will completely vanish in 10 hours).

What we’re about

We, like you, found ourselves working tirelessly into the evenings, being stuck visiting with relatives we wish we weren’t related to, and standing in line at Disneyland too long. Why couldn’t we have a body double fill in for us? Better yet, wouldn’t couldn’t WE fill in for us? So we did! It’s been working so well, we’re actually on vacation while our Doppel’s are typing this copy and completing our launch.

How we made this happen…

The science behind it was probably the easiest part of development. During our first-ever attempt to make this happen, we accidentally opened a portal to the future and out dropped our completed product. After a night of partying to celebrate our success, we got to work reverse-engineering it so we could mass-produce and bring this to homes far and wide. We’re in the final testing phase, so start planning you’re next vacation!
